My name is Peter Kim. I live in Austin with my cat, Mr. Rogers,
though I typically just call him Freddie. He's a dusty orange-
colored domestic shorthair who showed up in my back patio one
day while I was living Florida, back in 2020.

I used to be a software engineer at
[chess.com](https://www.chess.com), the world's #1 chess
website, although I suck at chess. If you play chess, feel
free to friend me
[here](https://www.chess.com/member/the_blunderbus).

Now, however, I'm a substitute teacher for Austin ISD, and a
proud member of Education Austin, the union for district workers.
I'm planning on becoming a full-time teacher eventually, though
at the moment, I'm substituting to try to get an idea of how I'll
actually do in the classroom and see which subjects and grades
seem to fit better with me. So far, it's almost all been middle-
schoolers, which, uh, I can tell you about.
